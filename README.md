# 💫 About Me:
✨ About Me

Hi, I’m Khushi 👋
I’m a Computer Science (AI) undergraduate at IGDTUW who loves building things that actually work in the real world — from AI research systems to scalable full-stack platforms.

🔭 What I’m doing right now
I’m currently working as a Research Intern at DRDO (Scientific Analysis Group), where I’m developing YOLO-based computer vision pipelines to study model accuracy, hallucinations, and human misclassification across large-scale datasets.

🌱 What I’m growing into
I’m deeply interested in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and System Design, and I actively sharpen my problem-solving skills through Data Structures & Algorithms in Java and hands-on project building.

👯 What I love collaborating on
Hackathons, AI/ML projects, full-stack applications, and ideas that start as a concept and evolve into impact-driven products.

💬 What you can ask me about
MERN stack development, AI-powered applications, real-time systems (WebRTC, Socket.io), DSA strategies, or how to turn an idea into a production-ready project.

⚡ A fun fact about me
I enjoy taking ownership of projects — from architecture to deployment — and I don’t stop refining until it feels right 🚀
